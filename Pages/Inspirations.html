Inspirations

We are delighted to share some inspiring quotes that celebrate
the beauty and significance of plants:

" The earth laughs in flowers"
" In every walk with nature,one receives far more than he seeks"
" The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now"
" A society grows great when old men plants tress whose shade they know they
shall never sit in"
" The love of gardening is a seed once sown that never dies"

So, whether you are tending to a lush garden or simply admiring a potted plant on your windowsill,
may these quotes inspire you to embrace the serenity and joy that plants offer.
