Chaparral

Larrea tridentata

The leaves and twigs are used by Native Americans to make a herbal
tea used for a variety of conditions, including arthritis, cancer and a
number of others. Subsequent studies have been extremely variable, at best.

Common hollyhock

Scientific name: Alcea rosea

Believed to be an emollient and laxative. It is used to control inflammation,
to stop bedwetting and as a mouthwash in cases of bleeding gums.
